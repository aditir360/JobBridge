Welcome

Welcome to our platform, JobBridge. The goal of JobBridge is to create an inclusive platform that connects individuals with disabilities to job opportunities while addressing their specific needs and challenges. Despite making 20% of the workforce, disable individuals hold only 4% if leadership positions, dindicating a significant gap in carreer advancement opporutnites. Therefore, it's vital to empower disabled people to find a work environment and community where they can thrive and grow without any barriers, bringing equality and justice to all.

How does JobBridge help?

JobBridge fufills it's goal of creating an inclusive platform that connects individuals with disabilities to job opportunities through it's 3 main features:



  • Job Search: A tool that allows users to efficiently search for jobs based on their skills and preferences using a feasible filtering system.


  • AI Chatbot: An multilingual AI Assistant that offers personalized advice and support. It also provides guidance for finding job opportunities and navigating the job market.


  • Library: A comprehensive resource hub offering a curated collection of websites, tutorials, and development tools needed for obtaining the skills needed for your dream job.


Vision for the future

JobBridge envisions a future where the job market is fully inclusive and accessible, empowering individuals with disabilities to thrive in their careers. We aim to create a dynamic platform that not only connects job seekers with opportunities but also fosters a culture of understanding and support among employers. By leveraging innovative technology and community engagement, JobBridge aspires to break down barriers to employment, ensuring that every individual, regardless of their abilities, has equal access to meaningful work.

Our vision includes a robust network of employers committed to diversity and inclusion, comprehensive resources for skill development, and a vibrant community where experiences and insights are shared. Ultimately, JobBridge seeks to redefine the employment landscape, making it a place where everyone can contribute their unique talents and perspectives.
